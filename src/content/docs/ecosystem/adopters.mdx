## Become an Adopter

We'd love to hear from you if you're using OJS â€” whether in production, staging, or development.

**Benefits of being listed:**
- Visibility as an early mover in the OJS ecosystem
- Influence on the standard's evolution
- Priority support from maintainers (for pilot participants)
- Featured in CNCF Sandbox application

**How to add your organization:**
1. Fork the [openjobspec](https://github.com/openjobspec/openjobspec) repository
2. Edit `ADOPTERS.md` with your details
3. Open a pull request

Or email **pilot@openjobspec.org** to join the [Early Adopter Pilot Program](https://github.com/openjobspec/openjobspec/blob/main/docs/case-studies/PILOT_PROGRAM.md).
